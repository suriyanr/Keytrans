VOWELS Assignment:
==============

a = a = அ (pulli in ')
e = e = இ (matra in ])
i = i = ஐ (matra in I)
o = o = ஒ (matra in [, followed by consonant, & /)
u = u = உ (matra in H for 5 Sanscrit  & Tamil consonants) Not used while writing Tamil consonants. *
ye = y = எ (matra in [)

aa = A = ஆ (matra in /)
ee = E = ஈ (matra in })
oo = O = ஓ (matra in {, followed by consonant, & /)
uu = U = ஊ (matra in BUCK3 h for 5 Sanscrit & Tamil consonants) Not used while writing Tamil consonants. *
yE = Y = ஏ (matra in {)

* NOTE: Hence the necessity for VUTAM, where u & U marked consonants can be typed in one go, w/o u or U vowel marks or matras. There are differances in writing Tamil and Unicode Tamil!!
